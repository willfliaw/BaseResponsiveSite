About Us

W Hostel is more than just a place to stay. We provide a vibrant community atmosphere, comfortable dorms, private rooms, and all the amenities to make your stay enjoyable. Whether you're visiting for a weekend or a month, we have a spot for you.

Our Rooms

  • Shared Dorm Room

    Affordable and social - perfect for backpackers.

  • Private Room

    Enjoy privacy and comfort with our single and double rooms.

  • Family Suite

    Spacious suites perfect for families or groups looking to stay together.

  • Common Area

    Relax and socialize in our comfortable shared spaces equipped with games and entertainment.

  • Shared Kitchen

    Prepare your meals in our fully equipped shared kitchen, open 24/7.

  • Laundry Room

    Keep your clothes fresh during your stay with our self-service laundry facilities.
